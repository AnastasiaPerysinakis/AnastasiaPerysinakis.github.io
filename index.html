I work on health systems, data portability, and patient-centred digital infrastructure. My interests sit at the intersection of healthcare, machine learning, and systems engineering.

I am especially interested in how fragmented records, delayed information flow, and poor interoperability affect continuity of care, particularly for mobile and high-need populations.
